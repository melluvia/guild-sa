About US

The Tech Academy for girls is comprised of talented tech mentors. We help girls feel empowered and inspired by leading classes and programs around technology.

Whether you are a girl, know a girl, or are a tech professional, we'd like to involve and include you in our mission.

- We are experienced.
When looking for an organization that you can be sure will deliever on its promises, it's important to inquire about experience. Here at TAG, we feature professionals with real-life experience working in technology- from small businesses to startups to enterprise and corporate environments.
+ We are multi-skilled.
From graphic design to UX/UI, to development and database architecting, our mentors do it all. Whatever your girl's skills and passions may be, one of our mentors is sure to assist and encourage her.
+ We are dedicated.
Whether brand new to technology or a coding guru, we work with each girl where they are at until they reach success.
